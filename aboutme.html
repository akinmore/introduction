My Story

When my voice is not too loud, I improve my actions. I was born in Jos, Nigeria and I was raised along with my four sisters.

I am a firm believer that Nigeria can become a better place, my mind fleets to ask how. I try each day to put those puzzles together.

Young man with big dreams for a nation but I have to accomplish these dreams for myself first, my family and my community.
